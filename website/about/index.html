About

WESONDER was created by Damiam Alfaro, an Engineer, Computer Scientist, Entrepreneur, and Project Estimator. After college, my goal was to diverge from the conventional path all my peers were undertaking and innovate in a new field. I wanted to endeavor into a different realm and explore the mechanisms of it; circumstances led me to encounter the Construction Industry, where I instantly fell in love with the field. Construction and everyone participating in this field are part of the backbone of society: Academic Institutions, Hospitals, Roads, Restaurants, Parks, and even the architectural structure you are in reading this right now is a product of this industry and its participants, ranging from Laborers, Operators, Subcontractors, Owners, General Constractors, Manufacturers, the list goes on. WESONDER is one of the many brainchildren in this field. I hope you utilize it productively.
