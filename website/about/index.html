About

Who?
WESONDER was developed by Damiam Alfaro—an Engineer, Computer Scientist, Entrepreneur, and seasoned Project Estimator.

After college, I decided to break away from the traditional path my peers followed and innovate in uncharted territory. Fate led me to the construction industry, where I found my true passion. Construction is one of the backbones of society—creating everything from schools and hospitals to roads, parks, and even the building you're in right now. This field thrives thanks to the efforts of laborers, specialists, operators, manufacturers, owners, and contractors. WESONDER is one of my proud creations, designed to empower and inspire your journey in construction.
How?
WESONDER was crafted through collaborative conversations with construction pros, turning industry challenges into innovative solutions.

WESONDER wasn’t just born from my passion for creativity and programming; it grew from countless conversations with construction professionals, understanding their struggles, and crafting solutions that work. From my time in the Accounting Department of a General Contractor to managing a $8 million park project in San Diego as a Project Engineer, and estimating over $25 million in awarded contracts as a Project Estimator, every role and conversation with peers has shaped WESONDER. The journey has been steep, but exciting, filled with invaluable lessons that I now pour into this platform to solve real-world challenges in the construction industry.
Why?
WESONDER advocates intercommunication in the industry, where collaboration fuels success, knowledge drives growth, and opportunities inspire a brighter future.

WESONDER connects invaluable knowledge of the construction industry’s key players with the collaborative efforts of its professionals. By uniting these forces, we create a space where opportunities flourish for everyone, fostering innovation, growth, and success for all involved. Welcome to WESONDER!
